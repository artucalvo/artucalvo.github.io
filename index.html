About

I am an award-winning technology enthusiast with extensive experience working in startups, corporations and academia in 5 different countries.

Early in my career I worked as a Software Engineer and IT Business Analyst in Ireland and Spain. Between 2012-2014 I founded three startups: Greenweaving (interactive diagrams generator), iFlikeU (Tinder for Facebook friends) and Dialective (interactive catalogs for retailers). With them I raised hundreds of thousands of USD in funding.

In 2014 I started working as a Senior Research Software Architect at ADAPT Centre, a world-class innovation hub based in Trinity College Dublin, where I led the development of research projects in the areas of Machine Learning, SaaS and Ethics.

After two enriching years in academia I joined Accenture and moved to Norway, where I am the Head of Digital Product Creation. In addition I hold leadership roles in the AI and Agile domains.

For 2.5 years I held a part-time CTO position in Sedicii, a World Economic Forum awarded startup that provides identity verification services. In the past I also founded XchangeUp (P2P currency exchange platform), Ethics Canvas (ethics collaborative brainstorming tool), Xtegos (funny voice generator app), two cultural platforms for Spanish Embassies and three popular meetup groups in Spain, Ireland and Norway. In 2019 I founded AImade.art, an online store that sells art generated by AI.

One of the milestones of my career was receiving in 2020 the Medal of the Order of Civil Merit by H.M. King Felipe V of Spain in recognition of my altruistic activities.

Passionate about technology and entrepreneurship, I am a recognized keynote speaker, seed investor and a lecturer in different innovation programs.

AImade.art

In 2019 I founded AImade.art, an online gallery that sells unique artworks generated by Artificial Intelligence, printed in canvas and shipped anywhere in the world. Limited edition: only one copy per design will ever be sold.

The algorithm is using Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) that, trained during several weeks over +20k public domain oil paintings, it is able to generate from scratch new portrait, landscape and abstract artworks.

  • Role: Founder
  • Dates: August 2019 - Present
  • Location: Oslo (Norway)
