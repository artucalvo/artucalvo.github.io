About

Arturo Calvo is a senior technology manager with extensive experience working with startups, corporations and academia. He is the Head of Digital Product Creation at Accenture Norway, leading an exceptional team that builds human-centric experiences for world-class clients

Early in his career he worked as a Software Engineer in Ireland and Spain. Between 2012-2014 he founded three startups: Greenweaving (interactive diagram generator), iFlikeU (Tinder for Facebook friends) and Dialective (interactive catalogs for retailers).

In 2014 Arturo joined Trinity College Dublin as a Senior Research Software Architect, where he led the development of research prototypes in the areas of Machine Learning, SaaS and Ethics.

For 2.5 years he held a part-time CTO position in Sedicii, a WEF-awarded startup that provides identity verification services. In the past he also co-founded XchangeUp (P2P currency exchange platform), Ethics Canvas (ethics collaborative brainstorming tool), Xtegos (funny voice generation app), two cultural portals for Spanish Embassies and three popular meetup groups in Spain, Ireland and Norway. Since 2019 he runs AImade.art, a gallery specialized in crypto art generated by an AI. He is also the creator of the memory game Kobadoo.

One of the milestones of his career was receiving in 2020 the Medal of the Order of Civil Merit by H.M. King Felipe VI of Spain.

Passionate about technology and entrepreneurship, Arturo is a recognized public speaker, lecturer in different innovation programs and seed investor. In his free time you can find him practicing outdoor sports or devouring a good historical fiction book. He was a basketball referee for almost 20 years. Arturo speaks Spanish, English, Norwegian and French fluently.

AI Art

Arturo has developed an Artificial Intelligence called Alicia that learned how to create art by analysing thousands of oil paintings from renowned artists such as Van Gogh, Goya or Munch.

After months of intense computing, Alicia understood the technique and patterns from the artists and she started creating brand new portrait, landscape and abstract artworks with her unique style.

The best artworks are minted as NFT on Ethereum's own blockchain, and made available for sale at AImade.art.

AImade.art

In 2019 Arturo founded AImade.art, an online gallery that sells one-of-a-kind artworks generated by Artificial Intelligence and minted as NFT on Ethereum's own blockchain.

The algorithm that Arturo adapted is based on Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN). After over 1200 hours of training using +20k public domain oil paintings, this AI was able to generate from scratch new portrait, landscape and abstract artworks.

AImade.art has been featured by Aftenposten, Re-Work, DND and Spansk Kultur, among others.
